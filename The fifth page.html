Did you know?/desert edition

Not all deserts have sands.

Desserts are scorching during the day and cold at night.

Desserts take up a chunk of the earth.

Antartica is the largest ice dessert.

The artic dessert is called the land of midnight sun.

The artic and antartica are refered to as polar deserts.

20% of deserts in the world have sand on their surfaces.

Desert land is growing each year.
